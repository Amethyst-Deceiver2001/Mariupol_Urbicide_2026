# Case Study — ZhSK "Brigantina" → no-tender lease to SZ "GSA Development"

**A pre-war private housing cooperative's land, already recorded as DNR state
property with no stated transfer instrument, leased without auction to a
Russian-registered developer building a subsidized-mortgage townhouse colony.**

Zhilishchno-stroitelny kooperativ "Brigantina" (ЖСК «Бригантина») was a
low-rise, individually-owned cooperative enclave on the Sea of Azov coast in
Mariupol's Primorsky district — structurally distinct from the Soviet-era
apartment blocks that dominate most of this project's case studies, since each
member held a discrete parcel rather than an apartment in a shared building.
On 16 July 2024, Denis Pushilin personally signed a one-page decree leasing
three of those parcels — 15,253 m² total — to a developer incorporated one
year earlier, for a 62-unit townhouse settlement marketed on subsidized
Russian mortgages. No compensation to the original cooperative members is
documented anywhere in the chain.

**Decree:** Rasporiazhenie Glavy DNR No. 297, signed Pushilin, 16.07.2024,
in force on the day of its own publication (§2).

> с ограниченной ответственностью «Специализированный застройщик «ГСА
> Девелопмент» о предоставлении указанному юридическому лицу в аренду без
> проведения торгов земельных участков общей площадью 15253 м²: земельного
> участка площадью 5919 м², земельного участка площадью 6167 м², земельного
> участка площадью 3167 м², расположенных по адресу: Российская Федерация,
> Донецкая Народная Республика, городской округ Мариуполь, город Мариуполь,
> жилищно-строительный кооператив «Бригантина», находящихся в собственности
> Донецкой Народной Республики, для реализации масштабного инвестиционного
> проекта, предусматривающего создание на территории городского округа
> Мариуполь Донецкой Народной Республики современного высокотехнологического
> поселения, состоящего из таунхаусов, «Коттеджный поселок «Бригантина»
> (блокированная жилая застройка) по данному адресу.»

**What it does, in three moves:**
1. **Asserts prior state title with no stated source.** The land is described
   as already "находящихся в собственности Донецкой Народной Республики" (in
   DNR state ownership) — the decree presupposes a reclassification from the
   cooperative members' Ukrainian-law title that happened somewhere upstream,
   undocumented in this instrument. **This bridging step — court ruling,
   administrative ownerless-designation, or otherwise — is not yet captured.**
2. **Bypasses competition.** "В аренду без проведения торгов" (leased without
   tender) — the same no-auction КРТ (комплексное развитие территорий)
   mechanism already documented for Rasporiazhenie No. 291 (RKS-Development /
   Troianda-M, see `docs/legal_mechanisms_review.md`).
3. **Names the end use explicitly.** Not social housing, not compensation
   housing — "масштабный инвестиционный проект" for a townhouse settlement,
   later named "Коттеджный поселок «Бригантина»" by the developer.

## Population-transfer framing

The chain documented here — occupied territory → extinguished Ukrainian
title (mechanism not yet captured) → no-tender land grant to a fresh SPV →
settler-housing product marketed on Russian federal mortgage subsidy — is the
same shape this project's other case studies document for apartment-building
stock, here applied to a private-cooperative footprint instead. It sits
squarely within **Rome Statute art. 8(2)(b)(viii)** (transfer of the
occupying power's population) and **RD4U category A3.6** (loss of access to
property in occupied territory): each of the cooperative's original members
is a potential claimant, and the decree's own text — naming the parcels, the
area, and the absence of any compensation provision — is a primary-source
anchor for that claim.
