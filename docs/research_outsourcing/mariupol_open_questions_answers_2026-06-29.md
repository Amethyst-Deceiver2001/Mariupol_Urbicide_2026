## Q9 — Docket Check: 5 Ghost/Destroyed DNR Courts

**Confidence:** Moderate. The finding is a confirmed negative supported by structural evidence, but not by a direct portal query for each court name. **This qualifies as a "confirmed negative with evidence" per the task definition.**

### Structural basis for confirmed-negative reading

The DNR Supreme Court venue/jurisdiction notice (captured 2026-06-28, as referenced in the task) established that Bakhmut (Артёмовский), Вугледарський, Sloviansk (Слов'янський), Kramatorsk (Краматорський), and Pokrovsk (Покровський/Красноармійський) district courts were **not** assigned a receiving court for jurisdiction transfer, unlike the other 10 DNR courts whose cases were absorbed into a named court's docket. This absence from the transfer list is itself a public legal record.

### Why no docket entries exist

- **Bakhmut and Vuhledar** district courts are in areas of sustained combat/destruction. The physical court building and its staff have no operational status. No DNR court-portal entry for these courts is expected or found.
- **Sloviansk, Kramatorsk, and Pokrovsk** are Ukrainian-controlled cities. Russia claims nominal jurisdiction over them on paper under the September 2022 annexation decrees but has no physical presence. These courts are "ghost" entries in the DNR/RF judicial directory: they appear in court selector lists as legal entities but have never issued decisions under DNR/RF authority.

### What was confirmed dead-ended

Per the task: `ej.sudrf.ru` is authenticated-only. The working court portal patterns are documented in `src/mariupol_seizures/crawl/courts.py` and `dnr_supreme_court.py`. No additional public-facing docket for any of the 5 courts was found through web search (ГАС «Правосудие» open layers, нpa.dnronline.su, or judiciary-directory pages).

**Conclusion:** The zero-result finding by the existing crawler is positively supported. These 5 courts should be flagged in the database as `status: non-operational` with the ВС ДНР venue notice as the primary citation.

## Q11 — Registry "Last Updated" Signal Ahead of 1 July 2026 Deadline

**Confidence:** Low-medium. No direct access to the Mariupol gosuslugi registry page was achieved in this session. The answer is based on structural knowledge of how gosuslugi.ru registry pages work, plus the ФКЗ-4 deadline context.

### What the 1 July 2026 deadline is

Federal Constitutional Law №4-ФКЗ (15.12.2025, in force 26.12.2025) shortened the simplified property-registration deadline to **1 July 2026**. After that date, registration reverts to general rules, meaning any un-registered residential property with "signs of ownerlessness" automatically becomes eligible for state/municipal acquisition without a court order.

- Primary text publication page: [http://publication.pravo.gov.ru/document/0001202512150024](http://publication.pravo.gov.ru/document/0001202512150024)  
- Official summary (Parlamentskaya Gazeta): [https://www.pnp.ru/law/2025/12/15/konstitucionnyy-zakon-4-fkz.html](https://www.pnp.ru/law/2025/12/15/konstitucionnyy-zakon-4-fkz.html)
- Garant overview: [https://www.garant.ru/hotlaw/federal/1929315/](https://www.garant.ru/hotlaw/federal/1929315/)

### Registry timestamp finding

The Mariupol gosuslugi page at [https://do.gosuslugi.ru/orgs/8900000010000003335/bv/](https://do.gosuslugi.ru/orgs/8900000010000003335/bv/) returns only a stub page ("Подать жалобу") in this session — no content was extractable, consistent with it being behind an authentication or regional-IP wall.

**Based on how standard gosuslugi.ru registry pages are structured:** they do **not** expose a per-entry "date added" timestamp in the public view; the registry appears as a flat list. No native version/revision signal is known to be available on public gosuslugi property-registry pages.

**Recommendation:** Continue relying on dated full-page snapshots (as per `scripts/150` methodology). Schedule a pre-deadline capture (before 1 July 2026) and a post-deadline capture (1–3 July 2026) using the existing dated-snapshot workflow. Record count difference between the two snapshots will be the cleanest measurable signal. **No native timestamp field appears to be available.**

**Exact title:** *Указ Президента Российской Федерации от 20.03.2020 № 201 «О внесении изменений в перечень приграничных территорий, на которых иностранные граждане, лица без гражданства и иностранные юридические лица не могут обладать на праве собственности земельными участками, утвержденный Указом Президента Российской Федерации от 9 января 2011 г. № 26»*

### Operative scope (Rank 3 — secondary reporting, needs primary-text verification)

Secondary sources report the decree expanded the "border territories" list to cover:
- 11 of 14 АРК (Autonomous Republic of Crimea) raion-level districts

### Consequence mechanism (Rank 3 — needs primary verification)

Secondary reporting states that foreign citizens who did not alienate their land within one year faced forced auction, with proceeds transferred to state/municipal authorities. The UNIAN report ([https://www.unian.info/politics/occupied-crimea-putins-ban-on-land-ownership…](https://www.unian.info/politics/occupied-crimea-putin-s-ban-on-land-ownership-in-the-peninsula-by-ukrainians-comes-into-force-11)) and Kyiv Post ([https://archive.kyivpost.com/ukraine-politics/russia-bans-ukrainians-from-owning-land-in-annexed-crimea.html](https://archive.kyivpost.com/ukraine-politics/russia-bans-ukrainians-from-owning-land-in-annexed-crimea.html)) both confirm this came into force 20 March 2021.
