How occupation turns a home into someone else's title

Each rung is a legal instrument. Together they move a Ukrainian-owned property out of its owner's hands and re-issue it — as municipal asset, as cleared land, as a new building at a new address, as a mortgage sold to a Russian citizen. The system documents itself; this maps where each act sits, and what evidence we still need.
